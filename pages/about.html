Hey there, I am a Ph.D student at Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IIT Gandhinagar) working in the NanoDC lab under the supervision of Prof. Joycee Mekie. I work in the area of energy efficient computing. I have worked on the design of approximate circuits for both integer and floating point operations. I have also started looking into the field of approximate architectures which support approximate computing.

I received my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology Meghalaya in 2015. I spent a semester in 2016 at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. I also spent a semester of 2017 at Intel, Bangalore and was working on On Chip Power Distribution Networks. I am currently interning with Prof. Onur Mutlu at ETH Zurich, Switzerland.

I have also been a teaching assistant for various courses like

  • Embedded Systems(Lab)
  • VLSI Design
  • Introduction to Analog and Digital Circuits
  • Microelectronics Lab
  • Embedded Systems
