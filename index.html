About Me

I am a Principal Research Scientist at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay). I am a memebr of the Computer Architecture and Dependable Systems Lab (CADSL). My advisor is Prof. Virendra Singh. I am working in the area of VLSI Design and Computer Architecture.

I received my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology Meghalaya in 2015. I spent a semester in 2016 at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. I also spent a semester of 2017 at Intel, Bangalore and was working on On Chip Power Distribution Networks. In 2019, I interned with Prof. Onur Mutlu at ETH Zurich, Switzerland. I have submitted my Ph.D. thesis and am open to opportunities.

I have also been a teaching assistant at IIT Gandhinagar for various courses like:
